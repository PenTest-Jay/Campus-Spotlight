Fit Series Boot-Camp

Date & Time June 8, 2026 | 8:00 PM
Location Hall Courtyard
Capacity 150 Spots

About This Event

Push your limits with our high-energy outdoor fitness session. Open to all students and staff looking to improve their strength and stamina.

What’s in Store for You:

  • The Power Hour: 60 minutes of metabolic conditioning that burns calories long after the session ends.
  • Pro Coaching: Real-time feedback from the University’s top athletic trainers to ensure you move safely.
  • Vibe Check: Live DJ sets to keep the tempo high and your motivation higher.
  • Recovery Zone: Post-workout cool-down featuring guided mobility and complimentary protein shakes.

Pre-Event Checklist:

To ensure everyone has a safe and productive session, please note the following:

  • Arrival: Please check in at the registration desk 15 minutes early.
  • Gear: Cross-training shoes are highly recommended for the courtyard surface.
  • Hydration: We provide a refill station; please bring a reusable water bottle.
  • Safety: A brief health waiver must be signed (included in the digital registration).
